About Us

Lúth is an innovative platform designed for GAA fans, players, and coaches, with a strong focus on AI-powered features to enhance training, analysis, and community interaction. At its core is the **AI Coach**, which delivers personalized advice, tailored training plans, and tactical insights based on user profiles, positions, and goals. Players can ask questions, receive actionable feedback, and access drills designed to improve specific skills like speed, tackling, or positioning. The platform also integrates AI into player analysis, offering detailed profiles with stats, strengths, and comparisons. Users can explore player databases and gain tactical insights powered by AI algorithms, making it a valuable resource for fans and analysts. Beyond AI, the site fosters community interaction through forums, gamification with points and badges, and live event updates with AI-driven commentary. GAA Hub combines cutting-edge technology, gamification, and community to revolutionize how users engage with GAA.
